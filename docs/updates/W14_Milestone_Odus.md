## Project Impact Statement

### Problem Solved
SecureHealth Chain addresses critical healthcare challenges:
- Fragmented patient data management
- High payment processing costs
- Lack of patient control over medical records
- Inadequate audit trails for compliance
- Inefficient cross-provider data sharing

### Solution Delivered
A blockchain-based platform that provides:
- 99.995% cost reduction in payment processing
- Patient-controlled medical record access
- Immutable audit trails for HIPAA compliance
- Encrypted, secure data storage
- Interoperable healthcare data exchange

### Business Value
- **For Patients**: Control, privacy, transparency
- **For Providers**: Cost savings, efficiency, compliance
- **For Healthcare System**: Innovation, interoperability, trust
